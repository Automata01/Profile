More About Me

One of my favorite things is sitting down with a cup of coffee to code and design. I started seven years ago, learning the basics of building websites, but quickly realized they looked plain. That’s when I decided to dive into design, balancing both coding and aesthetics to create something functional and visually appealing.

It’s not always easy there are days when I hit a wall, feel bored, or just can’t seem to get things right, especially without coffee to keep me going. But the satisfaction of seeing my work come together, whether it’s a sleek design or a piece of code that runs perfectly, makes it all worth it. I love how coding and design blend logic and creativity, letting me express myself while constantly learning and growing. It’s challenging, but it’s also a lot of fun.

Retrofee

E-commerce

Retrofee is an online coffee shop designed with a minimalist aesthetic, offering a seamless and intuitive user experience. The platform caters to three primary user roles: Customer, Employee, and Admin. Each role has specific functionalities tailored to their needs, ensuring efficient order management, status tracking, and sales analysis.

Travel Tour

Booking

Travel Tour is a booking system that helps you plan your perfect getaway. Choose from beach, mountain, culture, or town destinations, and customize your trip based on your budget, group size, and travel dates. We handle the details, creating a personalized itinerary for a stress-free and memorable vacation.

Log Emotion

Custom

Log Emotion is a simple and thoughtful app for tracking your daily moods. You can log how you’re feeling each day, and it helps you see patterns over weeks and months. Whether you’re reflecting on a tough day or celebrating a great one, it’s designed to help you understand your emotions and grow over time.

ELP

Web Design

East London Printers is a modern and visually captivating web design project created with Figma. It’s all about making the printing business shine online, with a clean, easy-to-use layout that lets visitors quickly find what they need. The design is professional yet approachable, focusing on a smooth and enjoyable experience for anyone exploring their services.
